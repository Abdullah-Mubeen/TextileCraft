Our Family History

1971

PakChii is founded, starting with local manual machines for woven labels.

Our journey began with a small workshop and a big dream. We started crafting woven labels using traditional methods, laying the foundation for what would become a global brand.

Expansion

Gradually increased our network and machinery, importing the latest computerized equipment from Europe.

As demand grew, we invested in cutting-edge technology. Our commitment to quality led us to source the best machinery from Europe, allowing us to increase production while maintaining our high standards.

Today

Strong presence in national & international markets, providing identity to various products and companies through a varied range of labels and badges.

Today, PakChii is a global leader in custom clothing labels and accessories. Our products can be found in stores worldwide, and we continue to innovate and expand our offerings to meet the evolving needs of our clients.
