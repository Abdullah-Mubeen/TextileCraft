Patch Types

Embroidered Patches

Our embroidered patches offer a classic, high-quality look with raised stitching that adds depth and texture. Perfect for uniforms, jackets, and bags.

  • Durable and long-lasting
  • Great for simple to moderately complex designs
  • Available in various thread colors

Woven Patches

Woven patches offer a sleek, professional appearance with intricate detailing. Ideal for designs with fine lines and small text.

  • Smooth texture with no raised elements
  • Excellent for complex designs and gradients
  • Thinner and more flexible than embroidered patches

Printed Patches

Printed patches offer unlimited color options and photo-realistic quality. Perfect for complex designs, photographs, and full-color logos.

  • Vibrant, full-color designs
  • Ideal for photorealistic images
  • Quick production time
